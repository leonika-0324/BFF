Builders of Faith Foundation (BFF)

Builders of Faith Foundation (BFF) is a faith-based community of civil engineers, architects, and construction professionals who are passionate about integrating values into their vocation. We believe that every structure we design and build reflects the wisdom, order, and strength of our Creator.

Our group serves as a spiritual support system and a platform for fellowship, professional growth, and service. Whether designing bridges, constructing buildings, or developing sustainable infrastructure, we are committed to honoring God through excellence, integrity, and compassion in our work.

Mission

To unite civil engineers and construction professionals in faith, using our God-given skills to serve communities, build lives with purpose, and glorify God through every structure we help create—both physical and spiritual.

Vision

To be a God-centered community of engineers who build with integrity, uplift society through service, and design a better world that reflects God’s order, strength, and compassion.

Core Values

  • Faith – We build our lives and work on the solid foundation.
  • Integrity – We commit to honesty and excellence in both design and action.
  • Service – We use our talents to serve God and others, especially those in need.
  • Stewardship – We care for the Earth and its resources responsibly through sustainable engineering.
  • Unity – We collaborate as one body, supporting one another in faith and profession.
  • Innovation with Purpose – We embrace creativity guided by divine wisdom to solve real-world problems.
